--------------------
About The Game (1.1)
--------------------


Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire is the fourth edition to the Harry Potter
games. This game has a unique new way about it, instead of roaming around the
castle like you've been able to do in the other games, you now have to choose
a level and play it level by level. You can also play co-op with two other
friends.

After every time you collect a shield it takes you back to the main screen
and you must select the level over or choose a new one. After collecting
a certain amount of shields you unlock new levels. 

While in each level you will encounter many creatures you need to 
defeat to obtain beans. You use thease beans to buy cards that will boost
your stats and help you in the levels.

Characters (1.3)
----------------


There are three characters in the game, who are the three main characters in
the series. Harry Potter, Hermoine Granger, and Ron Weasley. You can choose
to play as any of them in the game but it doesn't really make a difference
who you choose in the begging. You'll want to try to even it out so that you
can collect enough beans for everyody to unlock all of their cards.

                          / Introduction (2.0) \
                          -====================-


Harry Potter is in his fourth year at Hogwarts, and his most exciting year
yet, it is the year that they will be hosting the Triwizard Tournament.
This tournament has been going on for the last 700 years by, Hogwarts
Durmstrang, and Beauxbatons. It is held every five years and was just brought
back by Dumbledore a while back.

The tournament consists of three challenges that test the students in many
aspects, the first challenge is a test against a dragon. They need to collect
and egg that the dragon is guarding. The second challenge is a rescue
challenge, each student must find a way to breath underwater for one hour and
collect one of their friends that are stranded somewhere underneath. The
third and final task is a giant maze that will test the students more than
anything else, they must fight their way through it to find the triwizard cup
that is placed somewhere in the maze.

The schools all arrived and they started setting up the three tasks that would
test the three students that were chosen by the Goblet, one from each school.
From Hogwarts, it selected Cedric Diggory, Fleur Delacour from Beauxbatons, 
and Viktor Krum from Durmstang. After it had selected the students it suddenly
flamed up again and spit out one more contestant to this legendary tournament..
..Harry Potter.

                       INTRO/QUIDDITCH WORLD CUP


Your first objective in this game is to run through all of the chaos and make
it back to the portkey before you are killed. You need to run to the right
and you will find some dugbogs, keep running and killing these alone the way
to make it to a short scene of Mr. Weasley casting wingaurdium leviosa on a
rock in the way. All of you must do this and move the rock to the side to 
continue on.

Follow the path until you come to another spot where you are going to have
to move a rock out of the way. Once all your players catch up move the rock
to the side and keep going killing anything in your way. A long will 
eventually be knocked down in front of you. Pull it towards you to move it
out of your way. You'll have to do this three times and then head into a
new area where a bunch of dugbogs will surround you.

After you defeat all of these and continue you'll come to a bridge with a
rock blocking your way on. Move this and then when you come to the turn 
watch out for the rocks rolling down the hill. There will be a pile of
rocks with a death eater on top.... oh no what are we going to do. Pull
the log out from underneath him and send him tumbling down the hill. Now
you'll see it, the legendary, most feared thing of all, the dark mark.
Head onto the bridge to get the portkey and end the level.

||                                                                           ||
||                   (3.1) DEFENSE AGAINST THE DARK ARTS                     ||
||                                                                           ||
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-



You start off by meeting Mad-Eye Moody for and extra Defense Against the Dark
Arts lesson. First you must jinx the bucket that will give you beans after you
hit it. Search the room you're in and jinx anything that is jinxable to gain
some extra beans. Once you are done go through the door at the front to the
next room.

You'll enter a room with a Dugbogs in it. Jinx it until it blows up and collect
all the beans, more will appear so just keep on killing them until they stop
comming, be sure to collect as many beans as you can.

The next room teaches you how to use the Accio Charm which you will be using
alot. It helps you summon objects towards you so you can collect things that
are out of reach. Use the accio charm to get all the things on the pedastals to
open a gate that contains a spell book. The spell that it teaches you is the
Aqua Eructo spell. This completes the first lesson.



Now it's time to start your second lesson. You'll have to extinguish all of the
fires in the room with the help of the other two. Be sure to collect everything
on your way through the room before enterings the next one.

In the next room we're meeting a new enemy, salamanders. Kill it by casting
jinx's on it and collect everything that is in the room before moving on. Now 
you are on the outside of the castle, move up the ramps leading to the top
tower and watch out for the spray fire than comes out of the walls.

About half way up you will come to a salamander fire. First you need to put
out the fire, then put out the salamander before you jinx and kill it. After
you have done this the gate will open and you will be able to continue up the
tower.

Now continue up the stairs and again be sure to watch out for the fire jets.
You will now come to the top of the tower, there will be many fires around
the outside of it and then another salamander fire in the middle. First put
out the salamander fire so they will not respawn, now work on killing the
two salamanders. Then put out all the fires around the side and the big fire.

Pull down the drawbridge so you can cross to the other tower. Collect the
chocolate frog after you cross then continue on your way up. When you get
to the top you will find two salamander fires this time. You know what to do.
Once they are all dead the gate will open and you will be free to grab your
first triwizard shield. These shields are the point of the game, you must
go around and collect these, there are 38 in all and the more you have the
more levels that open up.
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-



From now on you must select a level from the Pensieve. You can select a
level, view all of the information about the level like sub-quests, or
press O to access Folio Universitas to see all of the collectors cards
you have collected.

Also when you select a level you can equip cards that will boost your stats,
you can equip three to every character before you start.*Note* you can't
equip cards during triwizard levels.

*****NOTE*****
After you collect all the mini-shields the shield will appear near you or
if you restart the level it will be near the beginning.

                          (3.2) HOGWARTS EXTERIOR

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-


Finally our first level, there are some things you are going to need to know
before you continue on in the game. There are more than just the shields to
collect in this game. You also need to find gargoyles, dragon statues,
vanishing cards, and mini-shields to complete this game to its fullest. The 
gargoyls and dragon statues, and vanshing card can be found in the sidequest 
section if you're a perfectionist like me. Also I think the vanishing card can 
only be obtained after you have collected all of the triwizard shields in a 
level.


1.) Obtained by collecting all 10 mini-shields
   
   * Mini shield locations *

a.) Right by the staircase in front of you when you start the level.
b.) If you go by the stairs and all the way left you'll come to four rock in
    your path, throw them out of the way so you can continue on. You'll come
    to a place where you can go left or right, go left and continue on. Move
    the block to the ledge so you can move up then go around either side 
    blowing out the fire on the way around. When you get there go to the little
    path that looks like its leading to the water and use Accio spell to get 
    it. 
c.) Turn around and open the gate, extinguish the fire to get your third one.
d.) Move the box from where it is to the opposite side so you can climb up the
    tower. After pull the bridge down so you can go from the tower on the left
    to the one on the right and collect the shield.
e.) Go down the stairs to your left, go down farther and break the wall to your
    left and then break the fountain thats in that back corner to collect the
    next shield.
f.) Go down the stairs, down again, break the walls to your left, the break the
    walls heading down the left side until you come to the one with the fire
    salemander. Break it and kill the salemander then collect the next shield.
g.) Go down the stairs, down again, and break all the walls going on the right
    side. After that extinguish the first burner then continue and extinguish
    all of them on the way. You'll come to an area with first and salemanders,
    just kill everything and extinguish everything. Take the cauldron and break
    the support to the building in the back right corner to get the shield.
h.) Go down the stairs, down, break all the walls going right. Now you'll be
    able to see a fire burner to your left, a cauldron in front of you and 
    many statues. Break all the statues and you'll find it in one of them.
i.) Go down all the way and move the rocks, continue down and choose the right
    path. Go down and pull the drawbridge down. Kill the erkling then pull the
    next bridge down and collect the shield thats there.
j.) Keep going from there and pull down the draw bridge then keep going up the
    stairs till you come to an are with a dark detector right in the middle and
    kill the erklings. Go down the path to your left side and collect the last
    shield.



After collecting all the mini shields the triwizard shield will appear either in
the beginning of the level or close to the last mini shield you just collected,
thanks to RPGSanchez for this information.



2.) In the begining go down the stairs to your left and stack the blocks near 
    the wall and climb up. Go around the wall and collect the Shield thats 
    there.

3.) Go all the way down to the left past the rocks to where you can go left or
    right and go right. Follow the path and pull down the bridge. Fight 
    everything in your way and continue pulling down bridges and going until you
    come to the stairs that lead you to the top of the tower. Extinguish all the
    fires and kill everything. After its all gone the shield will appear in the
    middle.

4.) Where the last one was it should be there right when you go in, you could
    get these two in either order this one is there all the time underneath one
    of the fires you just have to put it out and collect it.

5.) Go down the stairs to your left and go down the other stairs farther down.
    Break the wall to your left climb up the wall and lift the box down to the
    lower level, go down and break all the walls to either side and move the box
    through the walls to the other end and place it next to the wall. Climb up 
    it and collect your fourth shield of the level.

6.) Head down all the way and move the rocks, then head left and move the block
    so you can climb. Go to the other side and open the gate. Move the
    block on the right to the left side and climb up. Move the bridge down and
    cross put the block thats there down where the first one was. Climb on top
    of it and go up to collect the next shield.

7.) Go down and break the walls all the way down the right side. Go left and go
    past all the fires to the area with all the fire salemanders. Kill of them
    to open a gate in the middle top of the screen. Move a cauldron into it to
    hit the support on the building. It will fall and you can collect your next
    shield.

8.) Head down past the rocks and go down the left path move the block and go 
    down either side, go to the area that looks like its going into the water 
    to the fountain and use the herbivicus spell to get the shield.
    *NOTE* You can't get this shield until you do the Herbology level.

                         (3.4) FORBIDDEN FOREST

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-


There are 8 shields in the forest but some you may not be able to get right now 
because you need the herbivicus spell to get them. This forest may be hard to
figure out where you are in the beginning but it is actually really easy and 
it is easy to find everything pretty fast.

1.) Obtained by collecting all 10 mini-shields

   * Mini-shield location *

a.) Right where you start there are two boulders to your right, lift them and
    collect the shield.
b.) From the start go to the left and then to the left will be a Bototuber,
    launch it so you can go then your will come up to another one. Launch it 
    into the poisonous mushrooms to get the second mini-shield.
c.) To the right of the last one is a tree trunk with the shield on top of it,
    use the accio spell to get it.
d.) Keep going past 2 sets of mushrooms and you should come to a ledge that 
    will be on your right. Take the cauldronand fling it into the Butotubers 
    that are blocking a path up at the top of the screen then run there and get
    the shield inside.
e.) Get out and keep going and the dragon will be shooting fire go up on your
    right and you'll find your 5th shield.
f.) By the octogon you'll find a fire in the fires your next shield.
g.) Go left where all the Bototubers are and shoot them into the poisonous
    mushrooms to find the next shield.
h.) Pull down the door and climb the stairs, there will be many fire jets, in
    one is the next shield.
i.) From the octagon go to the left and then turn when you can make a right stay
    on the right side and its over there.
j.)

Reset the level and the shield will be right near you.


2.) You must find the Skrewt which is south of the octagon all the way back to
    the area with a stump and 2 bobotubers in the area, shoot it into the 
    poisonous mushrooms at the top of the screen then continue. After you beat 
    the Skrewt the shield's in the middle of the area.

3.) To the right of the octagon it is flying in the air. You must go left and
    find the block, levitate it back to the shield and put it underneath the
    shield to obtain it.

4.) When your being chased by the dragon or following it whatever your doing,
    you cross a bridge and fight a Skrewt and after you beat it you will find
    the shield.

5.) You must find the dragon that is in the cage to get the next one. Head south
    of the octagon until you get to the area with the tree stump and two boto-
    tubers, shoot the bototuber to the mushrooms in the south and continue on.
    You'll eventually come to an area with the mushrooms on the right side of
    the area, shoot those mushrooms to find the dragon. Now the first shield
    in the dragon area is next to the cage. Move the box in front of the cage
    so that it can not shoot you then put out the fires that are next to it, and
    collect the shield.

6.) In the same area as the last is the next shield on top of the cage. Head to
    the right side and shoot a bototuber into the poisonous mushrooms heading up
    the hill. Then you must use the herbivicous spell on the roots to get the
    shield.

7.) Start at the octagon and head to the left side and follow it up past all the
    mushrooms till you get to the stairs where all the fire shoots out. Follow
    it and cross the tree stump the head right and go to the end when you find
    what looks like a broken building. Inside it you see the shield hanging 
    there and you need to use the herbivicous spell on the roots to obtain it.
8.) In the area with the Skrewt you must use herbivicous on the plants to climb
    up to another area with another Skrewt up there. Kill the Skrewt and the
    Vampyrs until the shield falls then collect it to get the last shield.

                            (3.5) TRIWIZARD TASK 1

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-


Gold:   3 shields
Silver: 2 shields
Bronze: 1 shield

This is the first of the three triwizard tasks you must endure in the 
tournament. You must collect the egg that is placed in the area of a dragon.
You start out on your broom flying away from the dragon that is chasing you
around the castle. You need to fly around the castle and collect as many bean
rings as you can. YOU DO NOT NEED TO WORRY TO MUCH ABOUT THE TIME SO DONT WORRY
ABOUT FLYING THROUGH THE SPEED RINGS. All you need to do is collect as many 
beans as you can and you will easily obtain the gold and collect the egg that
will help you in your next task.

                           (3.6) PREFECTS BATHROOM

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-


1.) The first shield is obtained after collecting the egg, but its going to be
    harder than just grabing it out of the bathtub.
    Take the cauldron and smash it into the broken wall until it breaks and
    head into the new area. 

    From the bathtub area break the steam pipes on the right side, be careful
    and don't touch the water shooting out right after this. Now fall down and
    then head up towards the top of the screen and climb up the platform there
    then climb up the two platforms to your left right after it. Pull down the
    bridge and climb up and move the water out of your way. Now head back down
    the three platforms you've climbed up and head to the upper path that is
    now available to you. Pull down the bridge in this next area and climb up
    into the new area. Move the two water faucets in this area then drop the
    cauldron down right behind it onto the pipes to destroy them. Now you can
    exit this area where you came in on the bridge and down and back around.
    Now fall down where you climbed up before and this time go down the right
    path, watch out for the things that are shooting out water then head up
    where the two fish statues are and go right head all the way down and into
    the next area. Head to the left side and follow it until you come to a
    bridge, use carpe retractum to pull it down then follow the path until
    you come to an area with salemanders. Now you can either fight them or
    just head to the bridge at the top of this area and pull it down, after
    you do this head across and fall down to the lower area. Head all the
    way until you come to an area with more salemanders, after you fight
    them just walk over to the edge and it will get pushed back into the
    bathroom. Head all the way back the way you just came and right before
    the door will be a bunch of erklings, either kill them or just run past
    them and into the bathroom to collect the shield and open up the rest of
    the level.

2.) Obtained by collecting all 10 mini-shields.
   
   * Mini-shield Locations *

a.) Break the steamer with the cauldron in the middle of the first area you
    come to after leaving the bathtub area.
b.) After coming from the bathtub area pull down the drawbridge at the top
    and fling the cauldron at the steamers at the other side of the area.
c.) From the bathtub area break the steam pipes on the right side, be careful
    and don't touch the water shooting out right after this. Now fall down and
    then head up towards the top of the screen and climb up the platform there
    then climb up the two platforms to your left right after it. Use the
    cauldron to blow up the steamer in the middle and use accio to get the
    shield.
d.) From the bathtub area break the steam pipes on the right side, be careful
